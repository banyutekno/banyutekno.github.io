About Us

PT Banyu Tekno Solusindo is a company that provides security technology services and solutions, established in February 2023. We focus on the installation and integration of CCTV systems, access control, and security system consulting and designed to meet the needs of various sectors, including residential, commercial, industrial, and government institutions.

With a commitment to excellent service and competitive pricing, PT Banyu Tekno Solusindo stands as a trusted partner in creating safer and more efficient environments through integrated technology.
